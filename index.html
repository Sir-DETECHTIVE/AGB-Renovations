About AGB Renovations

At AGB Renovations, we bring your vision to life with expert craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a passion for transforming spaces. With years of experience in home and commercial renovations, we are committed to delivering high-quality results that exceed expectations. Whether it's a kitchen upgrade, a full basement remodel, or custom general contracting, we work closely with our clients every step of the way to ensure satisfaction and lasting value.

Reliable & Transparent — We pride ourselves on honest communication, clear timelines, and no hidden costs — so you’re never left guessing.

Skilled Craftsmanship — Our team of licensed professionals delivers precision workmanship, ensuring every detail is finished to perfection.

Customer-Focused — Your needs come first. From concept to completion, we tailor every project to suit your style, budget, and goals.
